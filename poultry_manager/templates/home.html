Key Features

Comprehensive Inventory Management

The platform provides detailed tracking and management of various inventory items, including feed, equipment, and supplies. Users can monitor quantities, costs, and purchase orders while generating insightful reports on inventory status.

Detailed Health Records Management

The system allows for the meticulous recording of health issues. Users can track health records over time, ensuring timely interventions and effective health management strategies, which ultimately contribute to the productivity and longevity of the flock.

Enhanced Production Report

FowlTrak's advanced production reporting capabilities enable users to track and analyze egg collection, sales, and production metrics. By recording and summarizing production data, the platform helps farm owners and managers make informed decisions regarding production strategies.
